About

Generations Contracting started because owner and operator, Kyle Wilson, has a passion for working directly with customers to make their dreams a reality. Continuing a family tradition of working in construction the team at Generations Contracting wants to bring their years of experience to make your projects the best that they can be. We have a team of dedicated estimators, project managers, drafters and tradesmen that bring together knowledge and experience from all areas of construction and design. We are dedicated to offering you the highest quality of work at the lowest possible price. If you have any questions about an upcoming project feel free to give us a call. We're happy to offer advice free of charge.

Owner Operator

Kyle Wilson was born in Carleton Place, Ontario and started working as a carpenter at 16. He worked along side his father and grandfather for many years building skills and developing his passion for contruction and design. This experience lead him to discover that his real passion lay in large scale projects. He persued this passion in the Contruction Engineering program at Loyalist College in Belleville, Ontario. Graduating top of his class in 2017 he was immediately able to secure a job as a Project Manager focusing on large scale projects. Since taking on this position he has truely learned what it takes to manage large scale and high value contruction jobs. He started this company to get back to his roots and bring his diverse set of skills to small businesses, and home owners as well as large corporations.
